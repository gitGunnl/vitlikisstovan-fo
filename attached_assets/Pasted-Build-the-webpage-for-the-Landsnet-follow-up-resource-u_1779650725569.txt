## Build these main sections

### 1. Hero section

Use the title:

**Vitlíki á arbeiðsplássinum: Haldið røttu kósina**

The hero should quickly communicate the central message:

Staff in public workplaces already use vitlíki through phones, work computers, and tools they already have access to. The real choice is not whether vitlíki will be used, but whether the use becomes deliberate, safe, and structured — or hidden, random, and risky.

Add a strong CTA that scrolls to the email signup section.

Use one of these button labels:

**Fá ritlingin**

or

**Fá tilfarið sent**

Include a calm visual metaphor. It can be a lighthouse, a path, a Faroese coastline, a small public-sector workplace scene, or a “right course” metaphor. Keep it subtle and lightweight, especially on mobile.

### 2. Shadow AI info box

Create a visually distinct early info box for:

**Hvat er skugga-vitlíki (Shadow AI)?**

This should stand out because it explains the central risk.

The tone should be serious but not alarmist. Use a soft warning style, for example warm pale yellow or sand tones with a navy border or icon. Do not use a red panic box.

The section should explain that staff may use vitlíki in good faith to solve work faster, but without formal training, permission, or safe tools.

Make it very readable on mobile.

### 3. Why act now

Build the section:

**Hví eigur tú at gera okkurt nú?**

This section should communicate that waiting does not stop vitlíki use; it only makes it invisible.

Emphasize the risk around:

- personal data
- citizen cases
- health data
- internal communication
- confidential information
- data leaks

Use this sentence as a strong highlighted pull quote:

**At bíða steðgar ikki nýtsluni — tað ger hana bara ósjónliga.**

The pull quote should be visually memorable, but calm.

### 4. Triangle section

Create a strong visual section for:

**Tríkanturin: Leiðsla, starvsfólk og KT**

This should show the three groups as a triangle or three connected cards.

The message:

- Leadership owns the goal, direction, and boundaries.
- IT creates the safe technical conditions.
- Staff find the real value in everyday work.

This section should be easy to understand at a glance. It is one of the most important parts of the page.

On mobile, stack the cards cleanly, but still show that the three roles are connected.

### 5. Seven steps section

Build a clear vertical section for:

**Sjey stig til trygga og skipaða nýtslu**

Use these seven steps in this order:

1. **Set leiðsluna í ábyrgd**

6. **Venjið tey viljugu fyrst**
7. **Gerið eina læringarskipan**

On mobile, these should be stacked cards with prominent numbers.

Add simple icons only if they improve clarity. Do not overuse icons.

A subtle path, route, stepping-stone, or cairn visual may be used if it fits the style. The section should feel practical and scannable, not dense.
